About Us

Darshanik Apparels has been operating from Tirupur — India’s textile manufacturing hub — delivering export-quality garments to international buyers and domestic brands. We provide design support, sampling, and full-scale production with transparent QA processes.

  • Design to delivery: Tech-packs, sampling, production
  • Quality certifications available on request (GOTS / OEKO-TEX / ISO)
  • Flexible MOQs and competitive pricing
